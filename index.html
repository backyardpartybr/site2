Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book and what is the process like?
1. Browse our Rental Inventory for tents, tent packages, tables, chairs, and linens.

2. Add items to your cart as needed - View the contents of your cart by selecting the cart icon on the bottom right of your screen

3. Select your rental period - if the items are available you will be able to continue to checkout.

4. Submit the information requested at checkout - be sure to read the rental contract, especially for concrete tent rental set ups.

5. Use code “Quote” at checkout to receive a quote OR leave blank and continue to payment. 25% of the order total is due to reserve your rentals.

6. Check your email for our confirmation, contract signature, remaining balance payment, and drop off/pick up details!
